COMMONWEALTH OF AUSTRALIAADMINISTRATIVE ARRANGEMENTS.ORDERED BY HIS EXCELLENCY THE GOVERNOR-GENERALI, WILLIAM PHILIP, VISCOUNT DE L’ISLE, the Governor-General in and over the Commonwealth of Australia, acting with the advice of the Federal Executive Council, hereby approve the following administrative arrangements in substitution for the arrangements in operation immediately before the date of this Order:—	1. The matters dealt with by each Department of State of the Commonwealth include the matters specified in the second column of the Schedule to this Order opposite to the name of that Department in the first column of that Schedule (except to the extent to which any such matter is comprised in the administration of an enactment specified in the third column of that Schedule opposite to the name of another Department) and other matters arising under the enactments administered by the Minister of State administering that Department.	2. The enactments administered by the Minster of State administering a Department are the enactments specified in the third column of the Schedule to this Order opposite to the name of that Department and any enactment (whether passed before or after the date of this Oder), not being an enactment specified at any place in that third column, that relates to a matter specified in the second column of that Schedule opposite to the name of that Department.	Given under my Hand and the Great Seal of the Commonwealth, this fourteenth day of February, One thousand nine hundred and sixty-two.										DE L’ISLE											Governor-General.By His Excellency’s Command,ROBERT G.
